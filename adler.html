Duration: Fall 2021 ~ Spring 2022

Collaborator: Chelsea Tang, Avani Guduri, Spoorthi Cherivirala

In the Fall of 2021, I begun a design project in an organization called Design For America in cooperation with national YMCA(Youg Men's Christian Association). We entered a CMU Design Fair at the end of the academic year hosted by DFA to present out project and won the best design award out of 10 teams by giving a short 5 min breif elevator pitch. In this project, I took the role of a UX researcher to build a mobile app that faciliatates professional career building foundations for youths in high school and their peers. This project aimed to foster the youth participatoin within the community by maximizing their impacts in local organizatios with the help of mentors from the platform.

End Product

An application compatible with desktop and mobile settings where users can create virtual 3D galleries and promote contents of their choice.

Organizing User Research

My team interviewed artists around the age early 20s and 30s to find the user need to create spaces in Adler. Also, These users were from the previous MVP of the product where they were only able to create simple 3D galleries with limited upload functionalities.

User research was done on phone as well as on zoom virtually with previous users as well as artists that we have reached out from different SNS platforms that mainly target artist growth and promotion. These services included Grafolio, Instagram and Deviant Art.

Stakeholder

Before moving onto developing prototypes for the service, our team generated stakeholder map and user persona.
Primary stakehodlers of the service included followings: Artsits and content creators interested in promoting their works online in accessible ways; and Gallerists or collectors who are restricted by money and time constraints to display works in a real-life setting or architecture.
Secondary stakeholders included the followings: Art appreciatros who would like to view works of their favrotie artists on line; and indiiduals in virtual reality who would like to view contents in an immersive environment.
